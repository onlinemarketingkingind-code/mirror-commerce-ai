# MirrorCommerce AI

## Vision

MirrorCommerce AI is an AI Shopping Concierge that helps customers make confident fashion and beauty purchase decisions using YouCam Skin AI and Apparel Virtual Try-On.

## Problem

Customers hesitate to buy fashion products online because they cannot visualize how clothing will look or determine whether products suit their skin tone, face shape, or personal style.

## Solution

MirrorCommerce AI combines:

- AI Skin Analysis
- Apparel Virtual Try-On
- Intelligent Shopping Recommendations
- Personalized Style Profile

to provide a seamless AI-assisted shopping experience.

## Target Users

- Fashion Brands
- Beauty Brands
- E-commerce Stores
- Retail Chains
- Consumers

## Goal

Reduce purchase hesitation and increase shopping confidence using AI.
