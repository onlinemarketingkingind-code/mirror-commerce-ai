# 1. Executive Summary


## Overview


MirrorCommerce AI is an intelligent shopping platform that combines **AI Skin Analysis**, **Virtual Try-On**, and **Generative AI** to help shoppers confidently discover products suited to their:


- Skin tone
- Skin condition
- Face shape
- Body type
- Personal preferences


Instead of requiring customers to browse hundreds of products manually, MirrorCommerce AI delivers personalized recommendations using **computer vision** and **conversational AI**.


The platform integrates **Perfect Corp.'s YouCam APIs** with **OpenAI** to provide an interactive shopping experience where users can:


- Upload a selfie
- Analyze their skin
- Virtually try products
- Receive personalized recommendations
- Understand *why* each recommendation is made


The primary objective is to demonstrate how AI can improve customer confidence while reducing purchase hesitation and return rates in online shopping.

# 2. Problem Statement


Modern e-commerce still faces several significant challenges.


## Current Challenges


- Customers cannot physically test products before purchasing.
- Many users purchase products that do not suit them.
- Beauty and fashion recommendations are often generic.
- Product discovery can feel overwhelming.
- High return rates increase operational costs for retailers.
- Existing Virtual Try-On solutions rarely explain *why* a product is recommended.


Consumers increasingly expect intelligent and personalized shopping experiences, yet many online stores still rely on traditional filters instead of AI-powered recommendations.


## Our Solution


MirrorCommerce AI addresses these challenges by combining:


- AI-powered computer vision
- Personalized recommendation engines
- Conversational AI
- Virtual product experiences


The result is an intelligent shopping assistant that delivers explainable, personalized recommendations.
